The Capital City is Brasilia

Lots of people think Rio de Janeiro is the Capital but it’s actually Brasilia. Other big cities include Sao Paulo, Salvador and Fortaleza.

Brazil is home to the 2nd longest river in the world

The Amazon river flows through Brazil and is the 2nd longest river in the world (the Nile is the longest). It is home to lots of animals including the Amazon river dolphin, electric eel and the green anaconda snake.

The Brazilian flag has 27 stars on it

The Brazilian flag is green with a yellow diamond in the center filled with a blue globe. In the blue globe are 27 stars and a white banner with the country’s slogan, ‘Ordem e Progresso’, which means Order and Progress.
